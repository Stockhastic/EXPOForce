Scroll Reveal Animations

Scroll Reveal Animation Types

Fade Up

Element enters from bottom with upward motion and opacity fade.

Use for: Bottom-aligned cards, footer content, closing CTAs

Fade Down

Element enters from top with downward motion and opacity fade.

Use for: Hero text, top-aligned cards, section headers

Fade Left

Element enters from right with leftward motion and opacity fade.

Use for: Right-column content, right-aligned cards, secondary visuals

Fade Right

Element enters from left with rightward motion and opacity fade.

Use for: Left-column content, left-aligned cards, primary visuals

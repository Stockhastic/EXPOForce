# PAGEINFO.MD

## Page purpose
Create a strong and trustworthy About page for a fulfillment/logistics company based in Armenia.

The page should explain:
- who the company is
- why Armenia is a strategic logistics hub
- what makes the company operationally strong
- what values define the working style
- who the company partners with

Primary goal:
- build trust
- position the company as a modern logistics partner, not just a warehouse
- show strategic geographic advantage
- make the business feel reliable, responsive, and internationally capable

## Target audience
- e-commerce brands
- marketplace sellers
- import/export businesses
- companies that need fulfillment and cross-border logistics between Europe, CIS, and the Middle East
- clients looking for a dependable operating partner in Armenia

## Tone of voice
- confident
- professional
- modern
- operationally credible
- clear and business-oriented
- no fluff
- no exaggerated geopolitical claims
- no vague corporate clichés

## Important instruction
Use this file as a structured brief, not as final copy.

Do NOT copy the reference wording literally.
Rewrite the ideas in fresh, original, natural language.
You may improve the structure, strengthen transitions, add supporting copy, and make the page feel more polished and complete.

## Do not invent
Do not invent:
- fake history of the company
- unsupported market leadership claims
- unverified numbers, years, capacities, or coverage
- partnerships that are not explicitly provided
- certifications, awards, or offices unless confirmed elsewhere in the project

If exact data is missing, keep the wording credible and neutral.

## Non-negotiable page structure
The page must include at least these 3 main sections:
1. About Company
2. Our Values
3. Our Partners

You may also add:
- a short intro block
- a founder / leadership note
- a compact CTA near the end
- a small summary block reinforcing trust and contact readiness

## Section 2 — Our Values

### Goal of this section
Show how the company works with clients day to day.

This section should feel human, responsive, and operationally disciplined.

### Required value cards or content blocks
Create 3 distinct values.

#### 1. Personal approach
Important ideas:
- each project has a dedicated manager
- the manager understands the client’s stock specifics in detail
- they do not just coordinate shipments
- they understand SKU-level realities and replenishment timing, including seasonal demand

Meaning to preserve:
The service should feel hands-on and informed, not generic.

#### 2. Always-on communication
Important ideas:
- the team understands e-commerce pace
- communication continues in Telegram
- critical issues are not postponed unnecessarily
- responsiveness is part of the service

Important note:
Keep this believable.
Do not promise impossible 24/7 support language unless supported elsewhere.
Frame it as high responsiveness and practical availability.

#### 3. Culture of optimization
Important ideas:
- the company constantly looks for ways to improve speed and reduce cost
- regular process audits help improve efficiency
- operational refinement is ongoing, not one-time

Meaning to preserve:
The company should feel proactive and analytical, not static.

### Presentation guidance
This section can be presented as:
- 3 value cards
- 3 columns
- stacked blocks with icons
- short philosophy statements with supporting text

Each value should feel distinct.
Avoid repeating the same idea in different words.
